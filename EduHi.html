ABOUT USProviding Better Management

Welcome

EduHi is an initiave taken by a group of college students who aims at improving the standards of education and thus building a user friendly application for students, teacher and parents to reduce the labor and improve teaching and learning experience in schools. It is a global platform for every school to connect and take a step to focus on education rather on management.

EduHi helps to provide a transparent platform for parents to administer their child's performance and praise him/her for his/her good performances and support them in ther average ones. This not only helps to reduce the generation gap but also allows parents to take some time in their busy schedule and connect with their child's school.

Let's do schooling together..

Features Providing Better Management

Daily Homework

Never miss your homework. Consistency is the key to hitting your goals. Keep yourself updated with the regular homework and class work being done in your class even if you have missed the school or on a holiday.

Timetable

Quicker notifications regarding any amendments in the timetable allows both the students & their parents to get prepared for any upcoming tests and be punctual about timings and submission of tasks..

Assignment

Because assignments are not an option... Get notified about all the assignment & project work to be submitted prior to the deadline with all the details required...

Toppers

Odd ones out right here:- With their excellent performance throughout the academic session, these kids have managed to grab the top most positions setting a benchmark for the upcoming generations...

Quiz

Let the game begin. We're determined to let our students grow socially and mentally alongwith excelling in academics by organising quizzes based on grammar, competitive aptitude, mathematics and general knowledge..

Notices & Announcements

An opportunity will never knock your door twice. Grab all the interesting opportunities regarding competitions and tournaments & stay tuned for all the updates in academic and co-curricular activities.

Datesheet

An ultimatum to fasten up your belt of efforts and practice to score the maximum in exams. Not only for the students but parents as well, notification of the datesheet at the right time is must to plan the study schedule properly and effectively..

Achievements

The champions are here.. Meet our extraordinary gems raising themselves above the common ground by doing constant hardwork and dedicated towards their goals...

Rewards

A token of appreciation to boost your confidence.. Students will be rewarded by their teachers regularly on the basis of their performance not only in academics but also based on punctuality, behaviour, appearance and extra curriculum..

Communicate

Cultivating the student-teacher-parent relationship is considered vital to the development of schools as learning communities.Effective communication is a clear indication of effective education. It enables not only teachers but students and parents to communicate with each other without sharing contact details,maintaining their privacy and building a healthy environment
